Cơ sở khoa học

Jason Koop
Training Essentials for Ultrarunning (2nd Ed.)
Áp dụng: Periodization, long run theo giờ, 80/20, B2B, race rehearsal, D+ weekly target = 60-75% race D+ ở peak week. "Run more, slowly."
Scott Johnston, Steve House & Kilian Jornet
Training for the Uphill Athlete
Áp dụng: AeT drift test, 3:1, aerobic hours, AeT-AnT gap, strength periodization, vertical = independent training variable.
Steve Magness
Science of Running
Áp dụng: Phân cực, cross-training, unilateral strength, taper, micro-dose Z4 maintain threshold.
Renato Canova
Marathon Training: A Scientific Approach
Áp dụng: Specific periodization, step-back bảo vệ key session, race rehearsal, progressive effort Z2→Z3→Z4.
